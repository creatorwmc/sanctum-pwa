Strongest Candidates

  • V01: Classic Mandala
    Most universally recognizable. Simple, elegant, scales perfectly at all sizes. Timeless spiritual symbol that works across all traditions.
  • V10: Gradient Mandala
    Adds visual depth and warmth while staying simple. The glow effect draws the eye and suggests inner illumination - core to spiritual practice.
  • V07: Seed of Life
    Sacred geometry recognized across cultures. More complex but distinctive. Suggests interconnection and creation - perfect for a practice app.
  • V09: Dotted Mandala
    Unique and modern. The dots evoke meditation beads/mala and the idea of practice as repetition. Distinctive without being tradition-specific.
